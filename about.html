About Me

Background:

I grew up in Illinois, but have lived in several different states. I currently reside in Chattanooga, TN.
I have spent the last decade of my life between retail, freight operations, and emergency medical services.
I have developed a passion for the tech industry over the last few years by creating designs and
layouts for streamers. I have also dabbled at creating projects around front office managing in baseball.
I'm probably one of the few people you'll know who can say they've driven an ambulance and can also create
the program needed for a patient's medical records. I have a unique blend of management and coaching experience.
I'm a strong believer in "leaders." Leaders lead by example and set the tempo and standard of what is to be expected.
Also great leaders create new leaders by teaching and mentoring those around them. This is how strong teams
are built from within. I've been a part of both winning sports teams and productive groups.

What led me to becoming a web developer?

I like having the ability to create things. When I sat down and looked inward I wanted to work
in a field with room for growth creatively. I've always created my own projects and events and
learning to code has helped to improve this and expand my skill set. I also like the ability to work
independent as well as in teams. I like the flexibility to work from home or in an office space.
I've always been a "jack of all trades" type of person, but never knew anything about building
tech and websites until now.

Goals & Aspirations:

day with collaboration from others to help in areas of sports, esports, and animal sanctuaries. I hope to
blend my passions together for the greater good. You can check out my current Github projects at this
Github Account. Feel free to drop me a message or
email me at brian.argetsinger@gmail.com
