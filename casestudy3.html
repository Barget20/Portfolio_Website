Streamer Portfolio Case Study

Overview:

The Streamer Portfolio was created as an alternative webpage that a streamer/ content
creator could use to show off all the important branding information they have. I
used myself as a sample demo.

Purpose & Context:

I wanted to learn some extra features using JavaScript. The click to reveal information
feature I found very fun and creative. Once I got that to work I realized I could use this
as a simple feature page for a streamer/ content creator. Most creators these days
use a linktree webpage. Other than changing the background color they are quite boring. I
wanted to increase the visual and interaction, as well as offering more than just links.

Objective

The objective was to build a fully functional single webpage to show off a streamer/ content
creator's information that you might find on their Twitch, Linktree, or personal website.
At the same time I wanted to keep it simple enough to update and use.

Duration:

This project took 1-2 weeks. I am not a designer by trade, so It took me a week to decide
on whether to keep the background images and style each font separately, or to just change
the background to a color when that specific panel is clicked on. Simplicity won out and I went with
a single color for each and a regular text style.

Credit:

Role: Lead Developer

"The How"

-An index.html file was created and sorted into five panels.
-The functions were created to open the panels and define what it would look like when active.

-With the help of a Wes Bos project I used his default setup for creating the way the panels looked
and were shaped. I then moved that information into the styles.css file.
-Once everything was working properly I updated the text, background images, and stylings. As I
previously stated this took some time to adjust how I wanted the webpage to actually look like.

-In the end I decided on a color, a different font type, and no background image when a panel is opened
I didn't want to make this project over complicated on the eyes.

"Challenges"

The major challenges for this projects are mostly through design elements. There is a lot CSS stylings
that are involved. There is also a certain limit on the paragraphs you can put in each panel. If you
don't put them in the right order the title will be wrong. Even now the title is not perfectly even
with the rest. There was a lot of trial and error on this projects to get the right design. It is not
perfect, but this is only a sample project page. The spacing between links within a panel is also an
issue I could not seem to find a proper fix for. Instead I just kept it to one outgoing link per page.
